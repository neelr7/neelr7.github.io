Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I change the values on the timer?

A: There is a gear icon through which you can modify the clock to your liking

Q: The Shot Clock won't load, what should I do?

A: If it does not load, there is a compressed link that will take you to the website that has the settings already saved for Lacrosse. It also has customization features, should you wish to change the settings.
